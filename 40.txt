Will of John Anderson - 15 Feb 1806

In the name of God Amen I John Anderson of the
County of Orange & state of North Carolina being of Sound
and Perfect mind & memory (Blessed be God) do this fifteenth
day of February in the year of Our Lord one thousand Eight
Hundread & Six make & publish this my last will and
Testament in manner following that is to say ----

First I give and Bequeath to my Daughter Mary one horse
named Darb two Cows two Fether Beds & Furniture

Secondly I give and Bequeath to my Daughter Pheby one Mare
named Mountain two cows two Fether Beds & Furniture

Thirdly I give and Bequeath to my Son Robert Forty four acers
of Land off the East end of the tract of Land I now live on
Including the land that he has now under fence also one
Feather Bed and Furniture

Fourthly I give and Bequeath to my Daughter Ann one Horse named
Lewe two Cows two Fether Beds & Furniture

Fifthly I give and Bequeath to my Son John all the Land whereon
I now live except the forty four acers above given to my Son
Robert with all the farming tools and waggon and Gears also all
my Houshold and Kitchen Furniture that has not been already
given also all my Living Stock of Horses Cattle Hogs & Sheep
I also allow my son John to maintain my wife Lettice in
a Comfortable manner During the term of Her natural
life I further allow my Son John to maintain in a Comfort=
able manner my above mentioned Daughters Mary Pheby
and Ann as long as they shall see fit to live with
him

Sixthly I give & bequeath to my daughter Lettice three
Sheep

[page 2]
Seventhly
I give and Bequeath to my son Stuart one Feather Bed

I hereby make and ordain my ["worthy"? x-out] Son[sic] Robert & John
Executors of this my last will and Testament in Witness
whereof I the said John Anderson have to this my last
Will and Testament Set my hand and Seal the day
and year above written           his
                             John (X) Anderson (wax seal)
                                 mark
Signed Sealed published and Declareed
by the Said John Anderson The Testator as his last
Will and Testament in the presence of
us who ware present at the time of
Signing and Sealing thereof

                               {   Thomas Armstrong
                        Jurant {
                               {   Robert Anderson
                                  William Anderson

              Exrs. Qualifd.
