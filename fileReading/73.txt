Will of John Linch - 19 Jul 1809

In the name of God Amen this Nineteenth day of
July in the year of our Lord one Thousand eight hundred
and nine ----

    I John Linch of Orange County & State of North
Carolina being very weak of Body but of perfect and
sound memory thanks be given unto Almighty God
for the same and knowing that it is appointed for
all men Once to die I do hereby constitute Ratefie
and confirm this my last Will & Testament ~~~

That is to say) I first of all Recommend my soul
and Body Into the hand of Almighty God who first
gave them to be Buried in a Christian like manner
at the Discretion of my Executors hopeing at the General
Resurrection of the Dead to receive the full forgiveness of
all my sins Through Jesus Christ our lord ~~~~

And I do hereby give and Dispose of my property as
followeth after my decease I desire my two sons Jackson
Linch & Cleaton Linch to have my plantation with all the
land I possess I desire also that my two Daughters Rachel
Linch & Gillie Linch should have their homes unmolested
as long as they continue Single on the said premises ~~

I also give unto my Son Jackson Linch a Good feather
Bed & furniture also one Gray Mair

I also give unto my Son Darling Linches heirs Two Dollars ~~

I also give unto my son John Linch Two Dollars

I also give unto my Daughter Sarah Jinkens Two Dollars

I also give unto my Daughter Frances Lewis Two Dollars

I also give unto my Daughter Olive Horner Two Dollars

I desire that all my Just debts should be paid and after the
Legetees[sic] have got their Legacys for all the rest such as a Good
Horse with my stock of Cattle & hogs with all and Singular
my household property with my crop of corn and Wheat
with all my working tools plows & hoes & axes

[page 2]
To be Equelly Devided Between Jackson Linch and Rachel
Linch and Gillee Linch ~~~~

I do appoint James Latta and my son Jackson Linch
as my Executors of this my last Will & Testament
Revoking and Disannuling all other and former
Wills & Testaments by me made ~~~~

As Witness I have hereunto sett my hand and affixed
my seal the day & year first above written ~~~

Signed Sealed and
Delivered in the presence of       John Linch Senr (Seal)
Henry Bunch  Jurat
       her
Rachel (X) Linch
       mark                  Jackson Lynch Senr[?] qualifd
       her
Gillee (+) Linch   Jurat
      mark

                         Orange County November Term 1813
The Executor of the foregoing last Will & Testament of John
Linch decd. was duly proved in open Court by the oaths of Henry
Bunch & Gillee Linch subscribing Witnesses thereto & Ordered to
be recorded at the same time Jackson Linch one of the
Executors named in the said Will was qualified
                           Test
