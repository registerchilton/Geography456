Will of Jacob Johnston - 20 Jun 1822

    In the name of God, Amen, I Jacob Johnson[sic]
of the County of Orange and State of North Carolina
being of sound and disposing mind and memory
do make this my last Will and testament in man-
-ner and form following that is to say.

    Imprimis
            I give devise and bequeath to my
wife Avey Johnson; the tract of land on which I now
live which I purchased from Clement Hassey[Massey] con-
-taining one hundred acres, to her, her Heirs and
assigns for ever I also give to my said wife all
my household and kitchen furniture, my stock of Hogs
horses and cattle and in short all my personal Estate
of which I shall die possessed

            I nominate and appoint my said
wife Avey Johnson Executrix of this my last will and
testament sealed with my seal and dated this 20th day
of June in the year of our Lord 1822

                                 his
                           Jacob (X) Johnson (seal)
                                 mark

Signed sealed published and declared by the Testator
to be his last will and testament, in presence of us

[page 2]
who at his request and in his presence became
subscribing witnesses thereto ----

    Sanders Riley     Jurat
    Warden Riley
           his
    Austin (+) Wesson Jurat          Exrx qualified
           mark
