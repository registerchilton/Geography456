Will of George Pratt - 15 Oct 1851

In the name of God Amen I George Pratt of the
County of Orange and State of North
Carolina, being of sound mind, and in
good health - but knowing the uncertainty of
life, and the certainty of death - do make,
ordain and publish this as my last will
and Testament

Item 1st It is my will and desire that my Executor,
hereinafter named do pay all my just debts ~~~

Item 2nd I give and devise to my beloved wife
Nancy Pratt The plantation on which I live
for and during her natural life, and at her
death To my son Thomas Pratt and his heirs
forever -- I likewise give to my said wife Nancy
The Two following negro slaves Viz Nance and
Alfred during her life, and at her death to my
son Thomas Pratt, his heirs and assigns forever ~~
I likewise give to my said wife Nancy all my
crops of every description household and kitchen
furnature and all my stock of cattle, sheep and
hogs, during her life, and at her death, to my
daughter Jane Pratt, her heirs & assigns forever ~~

Item 3rd I give and bequeath to my son James
Pratt the two following negro Slaves viz: Henry
and Emiline, to him, his heirs & assigns forever~~

Item 4th I give and bequeath to my daughter Jane Pratt the

[page 2]
three following negro slaves viz: Joe, Mary, and her
child Sally. to her, her heirs & assigns forever ~~

Item 5th I give and bequeath to my Son Thomas Pratt the
two following negro slaves viz: Alexander & Caroline, to
him, his heirs & assigns forever ~~

Item 6th I give and bequeath to my Grandson George
Morgan Pratt, son of Thomas Pratt, my negro slave
Stephen, to him, his heirs & assigns forever ~~

Item 7th I give and bequeath to my Grandson John
Hart Pratt, son of Thomas Pratt, my negro Slave
Bill, to him his heirs & assigns forever ~~

Item 8th I give and bequeath to my son William Pratt, the
sum of Five Dollars ~~

Item 9th I give and bequeath to my daughter Polly
Woods, the sim of Five Dollars ~~

Item 10th I give and bequeath to my daughter Phebe
Bishop, the sum of Five Dollars ~~

Item 11th I nominate my Son Thomas Pratt, Executor of
this my Will ~~~

        In testimony whereof I have hereunto
set my hand & seal this 15th day of October 1851

Wits:
Henry K Nash  Jurat
                          George Pratt (seal)
Pride Jones

          Codicil to the foregoing Will

I George Pratt named as the Testator in the fore=
=going will, do hereby make this present Codicil,
which I do order and direct Shall be taken as a part
of my foregoing last Will and Testament, and
which will, in all respects exception wherein it is altered
or changed by this codicil, I do hereby republish
and affirm ~~~

1st I give and bequeath to my son William Pratt
my Negro girl Sally with her increase, instead of giving
her to my daughter Jane as mentioned in the fourth
clause of my will ~~

2nd I give and bequeath to my daughter Phoebe Bishop
my negro girl Jenny with her increase to her & her heirs
forever ~~~

3rd I give and bequeath to my grand-son James Pratt
son of Thomas Pratt my negro girl Julia with her
increase to him & his heirs forever ~~

4th I give and bequeath to my grand-son Thomas, son
of Thomas Pratt, my negro boy Joe, to him & his
heirs forever ~~

5th I give and bequeath to my grand-son Edmund R.
Pratt, son of Thomas Pratt my negro girl Edith with
her increase to him & his heirs forever ~~

In testimony whereof I hereunto set my hand & seal
The 27th of March A.D. 1861
                                   his
                            George (X) Pratt
                                   mark
Signed sealed published & declared by the Said

Codicil to the foregoing Will

    I George Pratt named as the Testator in the foregoing will
do hereby make this present Codicil which I do order and
direct Shall be taken as a part of my foregoing last Will
and Testament and which will in all respects exception
wherein it is altered or changed by this Codicil I do hereby
republish and affirm

1st I give and bequeath to my son William Pratt my Negro girl
Sally with her increase instead of giving her to my daughter
Jane as mentioned in the fourth clause of my Will --

2nd I give and bequeath to my daughter Phoebe Bishop my Negro
girl Jenny with her increase to her her &[sic] heirs forever

3rd I give and bequeath to my grandson James Pratt son of
Thomas Pratt my negro girl Julia with her increase to him
& his heirs forever

4th I give and bequeath to my grandson Thomas Son of Thomas
Pratt my negro boy Joe to him & his heirs forever

5th I give and bequeath to my grandson Edmond R. Pratt son
of Thomas Pratt my negro girl Edith with her increase to him
& his heirs forever

In testimony whereof I hereunto set my hand & seal the 27th
