Will of Nancy Robinson - 17 Feb 1843

In the name of God, Amen, I Nancy Robinson
of the County of Orange & State of North Carolina
being very weak and Frail of Body but of sound mind
and memory & knowing the uncertainty of life do make
and ordain this my last Will and Testament in
manner and form following, (viz) I hereby give and
bequeath unto Alexander Robinson all my Stock of
Cattle, hogs, Household & kitchen Furniture crop, provisions
on hand, rents whatever may be due and any and Every
Species of property of which I may die possessed whether
specified in my will or not, I hereby nominate and
appoint Alexander Robinson as Executor to this my last
Will & Testament, Given under my hand and seal this
17th February one Thousand Eight Hundred and Forty three

Signed sealed &c               her
in the presence of       Nancy (+) Robinson (seal)
                               mark
Jas. Holeman Jurat
                        Exr qualified

                Orange County May Term 1843
The execution of the foregoing last Will & Testament
of Nancy Robinson Decd. was duly proved in open
Court by the Oath of James Holeman one
of the subscribing witnesses thereto & ordered
to be recorded At the same time Alexan-
der Robinson the Executor therein name appeared
in open Court & qualified accordingly
                      Test
