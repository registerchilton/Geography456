Will of Elizabeth Blacknall - 5 Jan 1848

    In the name of God Amen

That I Elizabeth Blacknall of the
County of Orange and State of
North Carolina being of sound
mind and memory do make and
declare this my last will and testament
(to wit)

1st I will that my executor (hereinafter
named) shall first pay all my Just
debts out of the first money that comes
to his hands

2nd I give and bequeath to my daughter
Nancy Jenkins one negro girl Charlotte
to her and her heirs forever

3rd I give and bequeath to my Grand
Daughter Lucy F. Young one negro Girl
by the name of Matilda to her and
the heirs of her body forever

4th I give and bequeath to my grand
son John B. McClanahan the sum
of three hundred dollars to be made
out of my property not otherwise disposed
of

5th I give and bequeath to my son
Richard Blacknall three Negroes (to wit)
Willis, Harriet, and Julia and all my other
property not otherwise disposed of above
also all my Household & Kitchen furniture
all bonds moneys on hand all accounts and
every other species of property I possess of
any description whatever to him and his
heirs forever it is my will sd. Richard
shall dispose of as much of sd. property
as will pay the amount in 4th Item

[page 2]
6th I do hereby appoint My beloved Son Richard
Blacknall My executor to all intents & purposes
to execute this My last will and testament according
to the true intent & meaning of the same and
every part & clause thereof hereby revoking and
declaring utterly void all other wills & testaments
by me heretofore made

In witness whereof I the said Elizabeth Blacknall
do hereunto set My hand and seal this 5th day
of January A D 1848                }
Signed Sealed published            }
and declared by the said           }
Elizabeth Blacknall to be          }
her last will and testament        }            her
in the presence of us who          }  Elizabeth (*) Blacknall (seal)
at her request and in her presence }            mark
do subscribe our names as          }
witnesses thereto                  }

WmSon Parish Jurat
Jas A Russell
H. Parish   Jurat
                    Exr qualified

                  Orange County February Term 1848
The Execution of the foregoing last will & testament
of Elizabeth Blacknall decd was duly proved in
open Court by the oaths of WmSon Parrish & H.
Parrish two of the subscribing witnesses thereto
& ordered to be recorded ~~ At the same
time Richard Blacknall the executor
named in said will came into Court
and qualified accordingly
