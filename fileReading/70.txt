Will of William Lewis - 6 May 1793

In the Name of God Amen

I William Lewis of Orange County & State of North
Carolina being Sic and weak in Body but of Perfect
Sound Mind & Memory, do make this my last Will
and Testament Makeing Nul & Voyd all other will
or Wills by me heretofore made ---

Item my will & Desire is that all my Just Debts
be paid by my Executors as soon as Convenient
after my Decease & the Resedue of my Estate
Real & personal I Devise give & bequeath to my
Brothers James Howell Robert Charles Nicholas &
my Sister Jane Mereweather Lewis's to be Equally
Devided amongst them & Each of their parts I
Devise give & Bequeath to them their heirs &
assigns forever -

    I nominate & appoint my Brothers James
Howell & Robert Lewis my whole & Sole Execut
=ors to this my last will & Testament in Witness
whare of I hereunto set my hand & Seale this
Sixth day of Feby. A.D.m. 1793 ----

Signed & acknowledged }
   in the presents of }      William Lewis (seal)

Eliza. Ridley
Charles Lewis
Thos. Hines Jurat

Item my Will and desire is that all my Just Debts be paid
by my Executors as soon as convenient after my decease
and the Residue of my Estate Real and personal I desire[devise]
Give and bequeath to my Brothers James Howell, Robert
Charles Nicholas & my Sister Jane Mereweather Lewis
to be equally divided amongst them and each of their parts

assigns forever:

I nominate and appoint my Brothers James, Howell
& Robert Lewis's my whole and sole Executors to this
my last Will and Testament In witness whereof
I hereunto set my hand and Seal this Sixth day
of February A.D.m 1793 ----

Signed and Acknowledged }
In the present of       }     William Lewis (seal)
Eliza. Ridley
Charles Lewis
Thos. Hines Jurat

State of North Carolina }
  Orange County ~~~     } August Court 1793 ~~~~~
                  The Execution of the above lost Will
and Testament of William Lewis deceased was duly proved
in open Court the Oath of Thomas Hines one of the
subscribing Witnesses thereto and was Ordered to be Recorded
                                    A. B. Bruce CC
