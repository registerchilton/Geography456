Will of Charles McCauley - 24 Jan 1864

                        Jan 24th 1864
I hereby give all my property of every
Kind to my wife Julia Ann McCauley
during her life or widdowhood and then to
her two children William Wilson McCauley
and Betty Irene McCauley I desire her to sell enough
property to pay my debts.  I also desire
Joseph Latta Sr to be Executor of this Will
                        Charles McCauley

Wm. Strudwick    } witness Jurat
John U Hart[?]   }

[Will Book Volume G, page 420]
        Jan 24th 1864
I hereby give all my property of every Kind to my
wife Julia Ann McCauley during her life or widow hood
and then to her two children William Wilson McCauley
and Betty Irene McCauley. I desire her to sell enough
property to pay my debts I also desire Joseph
Latta Sr to be Executor of this will
Wm Strudwick }               Charles McCauley
John U Heart }

             Orange County Court Feb Term 1864
The foregoing last will and testament of Charles
McCauley was duly proven in open Court
According to law and order[e]d to be recorded
