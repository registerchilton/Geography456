Will of Lazarus Cate - 4 Sep 1802

In the name of God Amen this Twenty fourth day
of September in the year of our Lord Christ one
Thousand Eight hundred and two - -

I Lazarus Cate of Orange County and the State of North
Carolina being very weak of body but of perfect and
sound memory thanks be given unto Almighty God
for the Same and knowing that it is appointed
for all men Once to die I do hereby constitute
Ratify and confirm this my last will and Testament

(That is to say) first of all I Recommend my soul
and body into the hands of Almighty God who first
gave them to be Buried in a Christian like manner
at the discretion of my Executors hoping at the General
Resurrection of the Dead to receive the full forgiveness of
my sins Through Jesus Christ our Lord
and I do hereby give and bequeath of the Same in
the Following matter and form

first of all I desire that my Just Debts should
[be?] paid and all the Remainder [of] my Land Horses
Cattle hogs and Sheep with all my Household Furniture
I Give & bequeath unto my Loveing wife Margirit Cate
dureing her Widowhood and afterwards to be equeally
devided between my five Sons William Cate John Cate
Isaiah Cate Thomas Cate and Benjamin Cate -------
and I do appoint Cutbird Burtin & Henry Bunch as
Executors of this my last will & Testament Revoking
and Disannul[l]ing all Other and former Wills and
Testaments by me made as Witness I have hereunto
set my hand & affixed my seal the day and year
first above Written
                                his
        his             Lazarus (+) Cate (seal)
Cutbird (+) Burtin Jurat       mark
Henry Bunch
          her
Christian (X) Burton     Cutbird Burton qualif. Exr.
         mark

The Execution of the foregoing last Will & Testament of Lazarus
Cate decd. was duly proved in open Court by oath of Culbird
Burton one of she Subscribing Witnesses thereto & ordered to be recor-
ded ~~~ At the Same time Calbird[sic] Burton one of the Executors therein
named qualified accordingly ~~~~
