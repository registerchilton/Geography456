Bill of Sale from Graham Bell to William Nash - 20 Apr 1793

[Will Book Volume B, page 233]
Know all men by these presents that I Graham Bell
for and in consideration of the sum of Seventy pounds
Virginia money to me in hand paid the receipt whereof
I do by these presents acknoledge have this day bargained
and sold to William Nash a certain Negro slave of a
yellow cplection named Horace, which slave sold as
aforesaid, I warrent and defend to the said Nash
aginst the claim of all persons whatever Given under
my hand and seal at Hillsborough North Carolina
20th April 1793
Teste                    Graham Bell (seal)
J. Estes  Jurat

State of North Carolina }
Orange County ~~~ ~~~   } May Court 1793
                The Execution of the above [obligation in such" x-out]
Bill [of] Sale was duly proved in open Court by the Oath of
John Estes the subscribing witness thereto and Ordered
to be recorded
                                    Abner B. Bruce CC
