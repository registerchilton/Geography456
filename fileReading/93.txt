Will of John Burnside - 1862

I John Burnside of the County of Orange in the State of
North Carolina being of sound disposing Mind and Memory do make
ordain publish and declare this to be my last Will and Testament in
manner and form following that is to say:

First: I give and devise to my sister Ann Burnside all my
    lands with the improvements thereon together with all
    my stock of Cattle and hogs and every species of
    property and effects whatever of which I may be
    possessed of[sic], at the time of my death to her and
    her herrs[sic] and assigns forever

Secondly I nominate constitute and appoint my Nephew
    George A. Faucett Executor this this my last Will
    and Testament

        In Testimony whereof I have hereunto
set my hand and affixed by Seal this 22nd
day of March A.D. 1862

Signed & Sealed in the  }       his
presence of us          } John (+) Burnside (seal)
Geo W Bruce             }      mark
Stephen T Forest
