Will of Jesse ODaniel - 26 Nov 1842

In the name of God Amen I Jesse ODaniel
of the County of Orang ["Carolina" x-out] and state of
North Carolina doo this day will that all
my Just debts bee paid and then I
will all my property to my brother
Henry ODaniel both real and pursonal
to him and his hairs roat with my own
hand and seld with my own seal
November the twenty sixt a d 1842
                 Jesse ODaniel (seal)
test
    his
Wm (X) Odaniel jurat
   mark
William Perry jurat

[Will Book Volume F, page 175]
In the name of God Amen I Jesse ODaniel of
the County of Orange & State of North Carolina do this
day will that all my Just debts be paid & then I
will all my property to my brother Henry ODaniel
both real & personal to him & his heirs roat[wrote] with my
own hand & sealed with my own seal November the
twenty sixth A.D. 1842
Test                        Jesse ODaniel (seal)
   his
Wm (X) ODaniel
   mark
William Parry

                            Orange County Nov Term 1843
The execution of the foregoing last Will & Testament of
Jesse ODaniel was duly proved in open Court by the oaths
of Wm ODaniel & William Parry the subscribing witnesses thereto
& ordered to be recorded

Additional Comments:
Will Book Volume F, page 175
Recorded Nov 1843

Estate Papers comprise five pages and are found in a folder labeled "O'Daniel, Jesse (1843)". No family history found.
