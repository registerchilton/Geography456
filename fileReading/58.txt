Bill of Sale from Timothy Cain to James Cain for Negroes - 20 Feb 1781

[Will Book Volume A, page 212]
This is to Certify that I have sold unto James Cain
Senr. Two Negroes named Casteel & Lucy the property of
which Negroes I Warrant & defend to the said James Cain or
his Assigns against any just claim or Claims Give from
under my Hand & Seal this 20th day of February 1781 ~~~
Signed Sealed & Deliverd       Timothy Cain (seal)
in presents of

Jn. Kelley Jno. Cabe

        Orange County SS August Court 1781
The Execution of the within Bill of Sale was duly prov'd
in open Court by the Oath of John Kelley one of the
Subscribing Witnesses thereto, and Ordered to be Recorded
                        Test
                            J J Benton Pro C.C.
