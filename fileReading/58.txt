Will of Sarah Hopkins - 18 Jan 1864

                In the name of God, Amen
I, Sarah Hopkins, of the County of Orange, and State
of N.Carolina, being in sound mind, and of a
disposing memory, but considering the uncertainty
of my earthly existence, do make and declare this
my last Will and Testament in manner and
form following That is to Say:

1st My will and desire is that my Executor
hereinafter named shall procure for my body
a decent burial, suitable to the wishes of my
relations and friends, and pray all my just
debts whatsoever or to whomsoever owing
out of the monies that may first come into his
hands as a part of my estate:

I give and bequeath to my five Children Mary Can
dis, Lorenzo, Peternow, and my Grand daughter Sarah
Jane the tract of land whereon I now live to
be equally divided between them absolutely for
ever.

2 I give and bequeath to my daughter Mary one
Bed and furniture absolutely for ever.

3rd I give and bequeath to My daughter Candis
one bed and furniture absolutely forever

4th I give and bequeath to my son Lorenzo one bed
and furniture absolutely forever

5th I give and bequeath to my daughter Petternow
one Bed and furniture absolutely forever

[page 2]
6th I give and bequeath to my son Benton
one bed and furniture absolutely for
ever.

7th I give and bequeath to my daughters Mary
and Candis one bed and furniture to be
equally divided between them.

8th I give to my daughter Mary one fall-
ing leaf Table and one Cupboard.

9th I give to my daughter Candis one fall-
ing leaf Table, and also two other little Tables

10 I give to my daughters Mary and Candis
the remainder of my household and Kitch-
-en furniture to be equally divided be-
tween them.

11 I give to my son Benton my negro boy
Frank absolutely forever.

12 I give to my daughter Mary one horese,[sic] Cow
and Yearling absolutely forever.

13 I give to my daughter Candis one Young
Cow.

14 I give to my daughter Mary all my far
ming tools absolutely forever.

15th, I give to my daughter Mary all my
Corn, fodder, Shucks, & Pork that may
be on hand at the time of my death
also all my stock of hogs absolutely for
ever.

Lastly I constitute and appoint my brother
Benton Ray my true and lawful Executor
to execute this my last will and Testament
according to the true intent and meaning
of the same, hereby revoking all other
wills and testaments heretofore made
by me.

Signed sealed published and }
declared to be her last     }
Will and Testament, who     }
in her presence and at      }         her
her request subscribe       }   Sarah (X) Hopkins (seal)
our names hereto as         }        mark
Witnesses Janry 18 - 1864   }
    B. C. Hopkins  Jurat
    William Leathers

            Orange County Court Feb Term 1864
The foregoing last will and Testament of Sarah Hopkins
was duely proven in open Court according to law
and ordered to be recorded
