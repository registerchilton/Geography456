Will of Elizabeth Sharp - 19 Nov 1799

    In the Name of God Amen

    I Elizabeth Sharp of the Town of Hillsborough being
of Sound and perfect mind and Memory (Blessed be God) do this
Nineteenth day of November, in the year of our Lord One Thousand
Seven hundred and ninety nine, make and Publish this my last Will
and Testament in manner following ~~ That is to Say, first, after
my Just Debts and funeral Charges are paid by my Executrix
herein after mentioned, I give and bequeath to my Daughter Elizabeth
all and Singular my Real and Personal Estate to her and her
Heirs for ever ~~ And I hereby make and Ordain my
said Daughter Elizabeth Sole Executrix of this my last
Will and Testament hereby revoking all former Wills.

In Witness whereof I the said Elizabeth Sharp have to this
my last Will and Testament set my hand and Seal the day and
year first above Written

Signed, Sealed Published & declared      }      Elizabeth Sharp (seal)
by the said Elizabeth Sharp, as her Last }
Will & Testament in Presence of us       }
who were present at the Signing &        }
Sealing thereof  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ }

J Casey         }
Ca Campbell     } Jurat
James Yarbrough }       Executrix qualified

Will & testament in presence of us who        }

J Casey
Catlett Campbell
James Yarbrough
                                 Orange County February Term 1800
        The Execution of the foregoing last Will & testament of
Elizabeth Sharp deceased was duly proved in Open Court by the Oaths
of J. Casey, CaCampbell[sic] and James Yarbrough subscribing witnesses thereto
and Ordered to be Recorded ~~ at the same time Elizabeth Sharp
Executrix therein named qualified accordingly
                             Test
