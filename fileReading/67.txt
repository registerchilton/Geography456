Will of Mary Ray - 2 Feb 1857

         In the name of God Amen

I Mary Ray of he County of Orange & State of North -
Carolina being of sound & disposing mind & memory
doth make this my last will & testament

Arte  1   I give & bequeath to my sister Charlotte Ray two
          negroes Tempy and by Thomas

 "    2   I give & bequeath to my niece Cornelia Ball
          my Interest in negro boy James

 "    3   I give & bequeath to my sister Charlotte Ray my
          part of the plantation on which we now live
          also all of my stock of horses, cows, & hogs, also
          my crop now on hand also all of my household
          property of every Kind

          In testimony whereof I have set my hand
          and seal this 2nd day of Febry 1857
                                          her
Witness R. Blacknall   Jurant        Mary (+) Ray (seal)
J B Leathers                             mark

[Will Book Volume G, page 162]
In the name of God Amen! I Mary Ray of he County of Orange
& State of North Carolina being of sound and disposing mind and
Memory doth make this my last Will and testament ---
Art 1st   I give and bequeath to my sister Charlotte Ray two negroes
Tempy and by Thomas

    2nd   I give and bequeath to my Niece Cornelia Ball
my Interest in negro boy James

    3rd   I give and bequeath to my Sister Charlotte Ray
my part of the Plantation on which we now live, also all of
my Stock of horses Cows & hogs, also my Crop now on hand, also
all of my household property of every Kind

          In Testimony whereof I have set my hand and
Seal this 2nd day of February 1857          her
Witness R. Blacknall Jurat             Mary (+) Ray (seal)
        J B Leathers                        mark

                         Orange County Court February Term 1857
    The execution of the foregoing last Will & Testament
of Mary Ray deceased was duly proved in open Court
by R Blacknall one of the subscribing witnesses thereto
and ordered to be Recorded
                            Test   Geo. Laws CCC

Additional Comments:
Will Book Volume G, page 162
Recorded Feb 1857

Estate Papers comprise five pages and are found in the folder labeled "Ray, Mary (1857)". Benton Ray was appointed administrator 25 Feb 1857.
