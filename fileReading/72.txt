Will of Daniel Green - 22 Apr 1850

In the name of God Amen I ["Daniel" blotted out]
Daniel Green of the County of Orange
and State of Nor. Carolina being of
a sound mind and memory do make
this my last will & Testament in the
following man[n]er that is to say First that
my executor hereinafter named shall
provide for my body at my decease a
decent burial suitable to the usual
Christian form and pay all my funiral
expenses out of my estate & all of my
just debts - second it is my will & desire
that my wife Elizabeth have one years
support of provision out of my estate. and
Keep the property that she had when I
married her. 3rd I give to my Grand son
Paul Proctor my wagon and Twenty
Dollars in money - 4th I give & bequeath
to my daughter Winnie Proctor wife of
Sterling Proctor my tract of land whire
on I now live adjoining the lands of Harris
Wilkinson & others ["Together and personal" x-out]
and all my personal property of every
description - 5th and I do hereby appoint
& constitute Sterling Proctor my true
and lawful executor to this my last will
and testament in witness whereof I the
said Daniel doth hereby sett my hand and
affix my seal this 22nd day of April
AD. 1850

Signed & sealed in the
presence of us            Daniel Green (Seal)
Thomas Proctor    }
Harris Wilkinson  }  Jurat
Albert Wilkinson  }

                         Orange County Court May Term 1850
The execution of the foregoing last will & testament of Daniel
Green decd duly proved in open Court by the oaths of Thomas
Proctor & Harris Wilkinson two of the subscribing witnesses thereto
& ordered to be recorded At the same time Sterling Proctor the
Executor therein named appeared in open Court & qualified
accordingly             Test
