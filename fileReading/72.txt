Bill of Sale from John Mitchell to James Comb - 30 Nov 1791

[Will Book Volume B, page 130]
State of North Carolina }
Orange County           }
        Know all men by these presents that I
John Mitchell of the County & State aforesaid have
bargained sold and delivered and by these presents
do bargain sell and deliver unto James Comb of said
County & State, four Negroes &c Joseph, David, Cate
and Sarah, for the Sum of four hundred pounds
currency the receipt whereof I do hereby acknowledge
I bind myself my Heirs Executors Administrator
and assigns to make such property good

In witness whereof I have hereunto set my hand
this 30th day of November 1791

    Teste        }    John Mitchell (seal)
John McKerall    }
Mary Comb        }  NB the above Negroes are those that
                    formerly belonged to John Mitchel Senr.

State of North Carolina  }  May Term 1791
Orange County            }  The Execution of the above Bill [of] Sale was duly
acknowledged in open Court and Ordered to be recorded
                                 Abner B Bruce CC
