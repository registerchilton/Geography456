Deed of Gift from John L Kirk to John L F Kirk - 20 Nov 1812

[Will Book Volume D, page 423]
November 20th 1812 Know all men by these presents that I John L Kirk
do hereby give to my son John L F Kirk a negro Girl Anaka to him
and his heirs forever, as witness my hand [and] seal the date above written
Sarah Kirk                                    John L Kirk

                                     Orange County February Term 1815
The Execution of the foregoing Deed of Gift was duly proved in Open Court
by the oath of Sarah Kirk a subscribing Witness there & ordered to be recorded
                                Test
