Will of Mary G Nash - 16 Nov 1860

I Mary G Nash of the County of Orange
and State of North Carolina being of sound
mind and memory, but considering the uncertainty
of my earthly existence, do make and declare
this my last Will and Testament, in manner
and form following, That is to say

First That my Executor (hereinafter named) shall
provide for my body a decent burial suitable to the
wishes of my children, and pay all funeral expenses,
Together with all my debts if any, out of the money
that shall first come to their hands as a part of my
estate

Item I give, bequeath and devise all of my
estate of every description both real and
personal in whatsoever it may consist, to my
beloved daughter Sally K Nash and Maria
J. Nash to be shared by them equally, absolutely
and forever ~~

And lastly ~~ I do hereby constitute and appoint my
sons Frederick Nash and Henry K. Nash my
lawful Executors to execute this my last Will and
Testament, according to the true interest and meaning of
the same ~~

In witness whereof I the said Mary G. Nash do hereunto
set my hand and seal this 16th day of Nov: A.D 1860

Signed, sealed, published & declared by }    Mary G Nash
The said Mary G Nash to be her last Will}
& Testament in presence of us, who at her request
and in her presence do subscribe our names
as witnesses thereto ~~
              W Strudwick
              Frederick Nash
