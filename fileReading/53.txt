Will of Darkus Hayes - 12 Mar 1808

[Will Book Volume D, page 261]
In the name of God Amen I Darkis Hayes of the
County of Orange State of North Carolina being of sound
and perfect mind & memory blessed be God do this 12th
day of March in the year of Our Lord 1808 Make [and] pub
lish this my last Will & Testament in manner follow
=ing (That is to Say) First I give & bequeath and here
by make & ordain my Worthy friend Ruthy Starks
Executor[sic] of this my last Will & Testament in Witness
Whereof I the said Darkis Hayes have set my hand &
Seal the day & year above Written I give and bequeath
to my friend Milly Beasley a blue habit and two streked
Coats and a pair of shewes[shoes]-- I give & bequeath to my friend
Nancy Martin a Tub -- I give to my friend Rachel Blan
chard a straked Habit -- I give and bequeath to my
friend Ruthy Starks two streked coats & a Tub --
I give & bequeath to my friend the Widow Starks my
cloak & a streked Habit --- I give & bequeath to
my friend Phebe Stark a white coat & some balls of
thread --- I give & bequeath to my friend Elizabeth
Starks a Calico Habit-- I give & bequeath to my
friend Christian Hurdle the res[t] of my property
Signed Sealed published &
declared by the said Darkis Hayes the
testator[sic] as her last will and Test.
who was present at the time of
signing & sealing thereof
Hardy Hurdle               her
James Hurdle        Darkis (X) Hayes (seal)
                          mark

[Will Book Volume D, page 262]
The Execution of the foregoing last Will & Testament of Darkis
Hays Decd. was duly proved in open Court by the oaths of
Hardy Hurdle & James Hurdle both subscribing Winesses
thereto and ordered to be recorded
            Test J Taylor CC
                By
                  Thos. Ferguson
