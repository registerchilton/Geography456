Will of Richard Gott - 30 May 1821

In the name of God, Amen. I Richard Gott
of the County of Orange and state of North Carolina
being in a low state of Health, but of sound mind
and memory. I make and constitute this my
last Will and Testament in manner and &[sic] form
following. -

1st. It is my will and desire at all my just
    debts be paid out of my Estate. -

2nd The residue of my Estate both real and personal
    I Will and Bequeath to my dearly beloved wife
    Catherine Gott and Sarah Squires daughter of
    Thomas Squires to them their heirs or assigns forever
    (Viz) two thirds to my dearly beloved wife Catherine
    Gott, and the remaining one third to Sarah Squires

Signed, sealed and acknowledged
before us to be his last will and
Testament. This 30th day of May
1821. -                                  his
                                 Richard (X) Gott (seal)
                                         mark
John Umstead
Thomas Gill
Thomas Squires } Jurat
