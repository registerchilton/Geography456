Bill of Sale from Timothy Cain to Mary Cain for Negroes - 20 Feb 1781

[Will Book Volume A, page 212]
This is to Certify that I have sold & delivered to Mary
Cain a certain Negroe Boy named Ishmel also a Child,
named Olafar the property of which Negroes I Warrant &
defend any just claim of Claims whatsoever
give from under my hand & Seal this 20th day of February
1781
Signed and Delivered in }       Timothy Cain (seal)
in presents of ~~~~~~~~ }

Jno. Kelley Jno. Cabe

[Will Book Volume A, page 213]

        Orange County SS August Court 1781
The Execution of the within Bill of Sale was duly prov'd
in open Court by the Oath of John Kelley one of the subscri
=bing Witnesses thereto, & Ordered to be Recorded
                        Test
                            J J Benton Pro C.C.
