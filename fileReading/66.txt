[Will Book Volume G, page 512]
I John Crawford of the County of Orange and
State of North Carolina being of sound mind
and memory do make and declare this my
last Will and testament in manner and
form following. That is to say

First That my Executors hereafter named
shall provide for my body a decent burial
pay all funeral Expense togather[sic] with my
just debts howsoever and to whomsoever
owing out of the money that shall first come
to their hands as a part of my estate

Item second I give and devise to my beloved wife Sarah
Crawford all and every part of my estate
both Real and Personal to have and to
hold to her the Said Sarah Crawford for and
during the term of her natural life

I do hereby constitute and appoint my wife
Sarah Crawford my Executrix and my trusty
friend and brother Samuel Crawford my
Executor to this my last Will and testament
according to the true intent and meaning of
the same and every part and every clause
thereof

In witness whereof I the said John Crawford do
hereunto Set my hand and seal this 21st day of
January 1867
                      John Crawford (seal)
Signed and sealed and declared by the said
John Crawford to be his last Will and testament in
presence of us who are his request and in his presence
and in the presence of each other do subscribe our
names as witnesses thereto
Abner Conklin
Michael Smith

    Orange County Court Feby Term 1867
The foregoing last will and testament of John
Crawford was duly proved in open Court See
minutes
