Will of Elizabeth Grimes - 24 Jan 1857

In the name of God amen
I Elizabeth Grimes of Orange County
in the state of North Carolina being
of sound mind & memory do make
publish & declare this to be my
last will & testament in
Manner and form as follows to wit

Item 1the[sic] I give & bequeath to my
nephew Hamelton Montgomery all my
land which is one Sixth part of
the Grimes track[sic] of land lying
on the Waters of Littel River it being
undevided containing one hundred and
fifty acres more or less all my personal
property Notes & [ink blot] Money after
paying all my Just de[b]ts I give all
the above named Item to my nephew
Hamilton Montgomery

Lastly I hereby Constitute and appointe
my nephew Hamilton Montgomery
Executor to this my last will and
Tes[t]ament where of I hereunto set
my hand & affix my seal this the
24 day of January A.D. 1857

Signed Sealed published
& declared in presence of
Wm Y McBroom   }                  her
Daniel B McKee } Jurat  Elizabeth (*) Grimes (seal)
                                 mark

In the name of God Amen I Elizabeth Grimes of Orange
County in the State of North Carolina being of sound mind & memory
do make publish and declare this to be my last Will & Testament
in manner & form as follows towit

Item 1st I give and bequeath to my nephew Hamelton Montgomery
all my land which is one sixth part of the Grimes Tract of land
lying on the Waters of Little River it being undivided Containing
one hundred & fifty Acres more or less all my personal property
Notes & money after paying all my just debts I give all the
above named Item to my Nephew Hamilton Montgomery

Lastly I hereby constitute and appoint my Nephew Hamelton
Montgomery executor to this my last Will and Testament
whereof I hereunto set my hand & affix my Seal this the 24th
day of January AD 1857    }
Signed Sealed published   }              her
& declared in presence of }    Elizabeth (X) Grimes (seal)
Wm. Y. McBroom            }             mark
Daniel B. McKee           }

                    Orange County Court February Term 1857
The execution of the foregoing last Will & testament of
Elizabeth Grimes decd. was duly proven in Open Court
by William Y. McBroom & David B McKee the subscribing
Witnesses thereto & order to be Recorded
                           Tet Geo Laws CCC
