Will of Aaron Boles - 22 Mar 1816

In the name of God, Amen. I Aaron Boles of the County of
Orange, And State of North Carolina, being of Sound and
Perfect Mind And Memory, (blessed be God) Do this 22nd day
of March in the year of Our Lord 1816, Make And Publish
this my last Will and Testament in Manner following that
is to say ~~  ~~  ~~  ~~~~~~

First I Give and bequeath, Unto Henderson Heany Ninty
seven & one half Acres of land lying on the Waters of Stags
Creek, Also 200 Acres of land in Burk[e] County on the Waters
of Middle Little River, As by Deeds will be shown, Also Unto
the said Henderson Heany, now About Two Years Old My
Whole Pirishable Estate Except One Dollar to my brother
John Boles, And One Dollar to Each of my Sisters, Also Unto
Nancy Heany my house And garden As long as she will
Stay and take care of said Henderson Heany, Also Unto
the said Henderson Heany One hundred Dollars Spicee

And I hereby Make And Ordain my worthy Friend
Walter Murray Executor of this my last will And Testament

In Witness Whereof I the said Aaron Boles have to this my
last will And Testament set my hand And seal The
day And year Above written,
Signed, sealed, Published, And       Aaron Boles (seal)
Declared, by the sd. Aaron Boles
the Testator as his last will And
Testament in the Presence of
us
    Walter Murray
    William Criswell
           his
    Robert (X) Fulton
          mark

The Execution of the foregoing last Will and Testament of Aaron Boles decd.

Fulton subscribing witnesses thereto & ordered to be recorded
                              Test
