Deed of Gift from Thomas Cates to Ann Durham - 29 Nov 1802

[Will Book Volume D, page 84]
State of South Carolina   }   To Whom it may concern I hereby certify
Newberry District         }   that I have given unto my loving Daugh
ter Ann Durham the Wife of William Durham in the State of North Caro
lina & County of Orange & her heirs that She had by her Husband William
Durham A Certain Negroe boy Named Sally and Incress that is now
in her possession for ever ~~~  by the Receipt hereof I renounce all right

[Will Book Volume D, page 85]
Title or Claim to the Same in Witness my Hand this twenty ninth day of November
1802 ~~~~~~~
Thos. Wadlington
James S. Gilliam                   Thomas Cates
William Bedire
Archibell Durham
                                   Orange County February Term 1803
The Execution of the above Deed of Gift was duly proved in open Court by
the Oath of James S Gilliam U William Bedire two of the Subscribing Witnesses
and ordered to be recorded ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
