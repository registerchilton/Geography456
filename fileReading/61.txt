Will of Jeremiah Glenn - 28 Jan 1814

Know all men by these presents that I Jeremiah Glenn of orange
County and State of North Carolina Do make this my last will
and testament in the following manner to witt I Give and
Bequeath to my son James Glenn fifty Acres of land to Be Laid
off adjoining the Lands of William Glen[sic] Mordecai Southerland
& Damery Wood to him and his Heirs for Ever. Item I Give and Bequeath
to my son Thos. Anderson Glenn fifty Acres of Land to Be Laid off
adjoining the lands of my Son Jameses land James Wood Fielding
Leathers. to him and his Heirs for Ever Item I Give to my Son - -
George Washington Glenn Ninety one Acres of land where on I now
live to him and his heirs for Ever

Item I Give and Bequeath to my Daughter Nancy Woods Heirs Ten
pounds Item I Give and Bequeath to my daughter Feriby Watsons
Heirs five pounds  Item I Give and Bequeath to my Daughter
Permilia Glenn ["thirty acres of land" x-out] adjoining the lands of
Feildings[sic] Leathers and Robert Glenn

Item I Give and Bequeath to my Daughter Polly Etheridge,s
Heirs Five pounds Item I lend to my Beloved Wife Betsey Glenn the
land and Plantation whereon I now live During her Nanural[sic]
life to do with as she pleases also one Womans Saddle and Bridle
The Ballance of my property which I have not given is to Be
Sold and Equally Devided between my wife Betsey Glenn and
the following Children (to wit) Kitey Horton Glenn Lucy Glenn
Permilia Glenn and George Washington Glenn

I do hereby appoint my Bror. William Glenn & James Latta Esqr
Executors to this my last Will and Testament In witness
whereof I have hereunto set my hand and seal this Twenty Eighth
Day of January one thousand Eight Hundred & fourteen
Test
Jono[?] Marshall                   his
Dancey Wood               Jeremiah (X) Glenn (seal)
         jurat                    mark
                    Wm Glenn qualified
