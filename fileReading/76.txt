Will of Thomas McCracken - 28 May 1838

In the name of God Amen ~~ I Thomas McCracken
of the County of Orange and State of North Carolina
being of Sound and perfect mind and memory ~~~
Do this the Twenty eight[h] day of May in the Year of Our
Lord One thousand eight Hundred and thirty eight (1838)
Make and publish this my last Will and Testament
in Manner following ~~~~

That is to Say I Give unto my Wife Susan McCracken
all my ["worldly" x-out] my[sic] property of every kind - after
paying all my Just debts, and paying One
Hundred dollars to my Nephew Thomas McCracken
(Son of my brother John McCrackin) ---

And I her[e]by make and appoint Thomas P. Paul
Executor to this my last Will and Testament  And
set my hand & seal the above day and year above[sic]
Written
                            }
                            }     Thos. McCracken (seal)
Signed Sealed published and }
decleared in the presence of}
                            }
Saml. N Tate                }

State of North Carolina } Court of Pleas &
Orange County           } Quarter Sessions
                          Aug Term 1845

The Execution of the last Will & Testament of
Thomas McCrackin[sic] was duly proved by the
oath of Samuel N. Tate the subscribing witness
thereto & Ordered to be Recorded
                            J. Allison CCC
