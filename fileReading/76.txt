Will of Katherine Piper - 19 Sep 1860

In the name of God Amen. I Katherine Piper of the County
Orange & State of North Carolina being of sound mind and
Memory but considering the uncertainty of my earthly existence
do this 19th day of September A.D. 1860 make and ordain
this instrument to be my last will and testament in manner
& form following that is to Say that my executor hereinafter named
provide for my body a decent burial suitable to the wishes
of my relatives and in the usual Christian manner and
that all funeral expenses be fully paid out my any monies
that may first come into the hands of my executor as part
& parcel of my estate

Item I give devise and bequeath to my two sisters Rachel Piper
& Sarah Piper during their natural lives and the lifetime
of the Survivor of them all my property real and personal
of every description of which I may be possessed at the time
of my death and after the death of the survivor of my
said Sisters my will and desire is all all[sic] of said
Personal property that may remain together with the Land
now belonging to me be equally divided between my
niece Sarah Strayhorn and George Piper share and
share alike to them their heirs and assigns forever

And lastly I do hereby constitute and appoint my worthy
friend James H. Pratt my sole executor to all intents &
purposes, to execute this my last Will and testament and
every part and clause thereof according to the true intent
& meaning of the same, hereby revoking all other wills and
testaments by me heretofore at any time made In testimony
whereof I have hereunto set my hand and affixed my
Seal the day and year first above written
Signed Sealed published and
declared by the Said Katherine
Piper as and for her last will and                   her
testament in presence of us who attest     Katherine (X) Piper (seal)
the same by her request in her presence             mark
and in the presence of each other
          S M Link
          Thomas Whitaker
          Joseph A Whitaker
