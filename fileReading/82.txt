Will of Samuel Thompson - 21 July 1800

        July the 21st 1800
I, Samuel Thompson of the County of
Orange and state of North Carolina Just
Account & less [legacies?]

Henry O'Daniel to Thirty Pounds Dr
Joshua Thompson to Eighteen Pounds Dr
Samuel Thompson to Six Pounds Dr

I leave to my son Joshua Thompson
son Joshua son of Mary Paynes
Twenty Pounds ~~  ~~  ~~  ~~
Samuel Bartly to have a Child's parte
Witness                       his
                       Samuel (x) Thompson
Thomas Bradshaw              mark

Additional Comments:
Will Book Volume D, page 127
Recorded May 1804

Samuel Bartly, aka Bartlett

Estate Papers comprise 15 pages and are found in a folder labeled "Thompson, Samuel (1812)".  This appears to pertain to more than one estate.  Testator's children: John, Samuel, Joshua Sarah Woody, wife of Joseph Woody, Rachel Hayes, wife of James Hayes, Jean O'Daniel wife of Henry O'Daniel, Anna Roach.  Testator owned real estate in Botetourt County, Virigina
