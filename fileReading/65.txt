Will of Elizabeth F Sawyer - 23 Sep 1820

In the Mame[sic] of God Amen --

I Elizabeth F. Sawyers of Orange County in
N.Carolina considering my mortality, & being
very week[sic] & feeble in body, do establi[s]h this
my last Will & Testament as followeth

first I give up my soul to God who gave
it and my body to the earth to be buried
at the discretion of the Executors --------

As to my temporeal[sic] estate all & every
part & parcel thereof real & personal I give
& bequeath to my Sister Mary C. Sawyer her
heirs and assigns forever And it it [is] My will &
desire that my friend Uncle John Campbell
be my sole Executor to carry into effect
this my last will & Testament, as
witness whereof I have hereunto set
My hand and affixed by[sic] seal this
twenty third day of September one thousand
eight hundred and twenty
                                  her
Sign'd seal'd and     Elizabeth F (X) Sawyer (seal)
delivered in presents             mark
    of
    E Turner    Edmd. Turner Jurat
    John Landers
                    Exr. qualified

                               Orange County February 1821
The Execution of the foregoing last Will & testament of Elizabeth F.
