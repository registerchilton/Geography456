Will of Mary Taylor - 13 Mar 1861

I Mary Taylor of the Town of Newbern
State of North Carolina being of sound mind
and memory, do make and declare this my
last Will and Testament in manner and
form following That is to say

                              I give and
devise to my daughter Elizabeth Ann Taylor
Eight hundred dollars ----

To my daughter Mary J wife of Martin
Stevenson of Washington NC Five hundred
dollars for her sole & seperate use free from
the debts contracts or engagements of the said
Martin Sevenson, husband of my daughter Mary
The balance of my real and personal
estate I give to my daughter Susan wife
of my son Richard N Taylor for her sole
& separate use during her natural life
free from the debts, contract or liabilities of
the said Richard N Taylor and at her
death to the children of the said Richard
N. & Susan Taylor.

And lastly I do hereby constitute and appoint
my son Richard N Taylor my lawful
executor to all intents and purposes

In witness whereof, I the said Mary
Taylor do hereunto set my hand & seal this
thirteenth day of March 1861

Witnesses           Mary Taylor (seal)
    Jos W Stevenson
    John L. Gardner
