Will of James Mullins - 25 Sep 1780

[Will Book Volume A, page 249]
In the name of God Amen I James Mullins make
this my last Will & Testament I Will & bequeath to James
Ross one Negroe boy named Toney also I give & Bequeath
one mare Colt as Witness my hand and Seal this 25 Septr.
1780
    Richard Clayton
          his
    James (+) Kanaday            James Mullen
          mark
             her
    Margaret (+) Wilson
             mark

[Will Book Volume A, page 250]
                Orange County ss. August Court 1782
The Execution of the within Will of James Mullin Deceas'd
was duly prov'd in open Court by the Oath of Richard
Clayton a Subscribing witness thereto & ordered to be Recorded
                          Test
                              J J Benton C.C.
