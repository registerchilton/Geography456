Will of Rebecca Dollar - 4 Apr 1845

    In the name of God Amen I Rebecca Dollar of
the County of Orange & State of North Carolina
being of sound and perfect mind and Memory
Blessed be God do this fourth Day April
1845 make and publish this my last will
and Testament in Manner following That
is to say first I give and bequeath
to my Daughter Susan Dollar all the Money
I have on Hand & all depts[sic] due me for pention
from the Un[i]ted Stat[e]s or in the Hands of
any other person for me

Secondly I give & beque[a]th to my grandson William
Glenn son of James Glenn my Bed & furniture
and I hereby make and ordain my Daughter Susan
Dollar executress[?] of this my last will and
Testament in witniss whereof I the said
Rebeca Dollar have to this my last will [and]
Testament set my hand and seal the
day and year above written given under
my Hand & seal 4th April 1845
    Test                 her
                  Rebeca (X) Dollar (seal)
J. Turner                mark
James Turner     [Exx] qualified

that is to say first I give and bequeath to my
daughter Susan Dollar all the money I have on
hand and all Debts due me for Pension from the

Secondly I give and bequeath to my grand Son
William Glenn son of James Glenn my bed & furniture
and I hereby make and ordain my daughter Susan
Dollar Executrix of this my last Will & Testament

In Witness whereof [I] the said Rebecca Dollar have

to this my last Will and Testament set my hand & Seal
the day and year above Written. Given under my hand &
Seal 4th April 1845                    her
Test                            Rebecca (X) Dollar (seal)
J. Turner mark  }
James Turner    } Orange County November Term 1845
                } The Executor of the foregoing last Will
and Testament of Rebecca Dollar was duly proven in open
Court by the oath of Jonah [Josiah?] Turner a subscribing witness
thereto And at the same time Susan Dollar qualified
as Executrix thereto And ordered to be Recorded
                             Test JAllison CCC
