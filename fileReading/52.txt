Will of Rosana Castleberry - 1 Apr 1860

I Rosana Castleberry of the County of Orange
and State of North Carolina, being of Sound
mind, but considering the uncertainty of earthly
existence, do make and declare this my last Will
and Testament, in manner and form following,
that is to say:

Item That my Executor (to wit) Ruffin Castleberry
Shall provide for my body a decent burial and
pay all funeral expenses together with my Just debts
howsoever & to whomsoever owing, out of the
money that may first come into his hands as
a part or parcel of my estate

Item I give & bequeath to my daughter Martha
Barbee wife of John Barbee the sum of One
dollar to be paid to her by my aforesaid Executor

Item I give and bequeath to my son Ruffin
Castleberry one Curtain bedstid one bed two sheets
and one bedquilt

Item it is my wish & my will that the
balance of my property of every description be
sold by my aforesaid Executor at public sale and
that the proceeds arising from said Sale be divided
Equally among all my Children (Martha Barbee
excepted) who is named above and that the
portion that my Children who are dead would
be entitled to were they now living shall be given
[page 2]
to their lawful heirs (viz) divided equally among
them.

    In Testimony whereof I have hereunto set my
hand and seal this 1st day of April AD 1860
Witness
A. B. Gunter  }               her
H H Blalock   } Jurat  Rosana (X) Castleberry (seal)
                             mark
