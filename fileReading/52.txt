Will of Jean McCanliss - 5 Feb 1806

In the name of God Amen the this[sic] 5th Day
of Feberwary[sic] In the Year of Our Lord
one thousand Eight hundred and six
I Jean McCanless of the County of Orange
and State of North Carolina being sick
and weak in body but of perfect mind and
memory thanks be to God ther[e]fore Calling
to mind the mortallity of my body and
knowing that it is appointed for all men
to die do make and ordain this my last
will and testament and as for my soul
I give unto the hands of God that gave it
And my body I Recommend to the Earth to
Be buried in a Christian like and decent
manner at the discretion of my Executors
nothing doubting but at the general ---
Resurection I shall Receive the same again
by the mighty power of God and as touching
Such worldly Estate wherewith it hath
pleased God to bless me with in this life
I give and d[e]vise and dispose of the same
In the fowling manner and form: - - -

Iprimis It is my will and I do order
in the first place that all my Just debts and
Funeral Charges be paid and satisfied: - - -

Itim I will and bequath unto John McCanless
One large pot and pot Rack and Chist =

Also to John Commons Senior Ten Shillings
Currency Also to James McCanless ten Shillings
Currency Also to Elizabeth Hamelton
Ten pounds Currency of the personel property
that may be found on the premises also to
David McCanless the Mansion house with
all the Improvements thereunto belonging
with one hundred and one Acres of land
Adjoining John McCanless on the south
and Thomas Jordon on the west

[page 2]
Also one Bay Horse And one Table
Also To William McCanless one hundred
and one Acres of Land Joining John
Porterfield and Daniel Baldridge
Also one Cow and Calf and ["pot" x-out] one pot
and pot Rack also one feather bed and
bed Clothes Also I appoint James H Bowman
and James Murdach my Executers
given under my hand and seal the [day] and
year above written
    Tests                   her
John Trimble  Jurat   Jane (X) McCanless (seal)
       her                mark
Marget (X) Trimble
      mark
      her
Sarah (X) Trimble
      mark
              James Murdock qualified
